Kieran Rosethorne

Introduction

Meet Kieran, the kind of person who can shred a guitar solo, whip up a gourmet meal, and drop a hundred push-ups before most people have finished their morning coffee. Equal parts musician and madman, Kieran brings the same wild, unstoppable energy to everything he does. Whether he's pouring his soul into a guitar riff, laying down a hauntingly beautiful piano melody, or perfecting a new recipe in the kitchen, he does it all with the same fearless intensity. When he's not making music or breaking a sweat with pull-ups, push-ups, and everything in between, you'll probably find him on the floor surrounded by puppies, because even the most chaotic souls have a soft side. Kieran doesn't just live life, he attacks it from every angle, and that raw, unfiltered passion is exactly what he brings to every note he plays.

Interests and hobbies

Playing Guitar

Kieran's relationship with the guitar is nothing short of a lifelong love affair. From blistering solos that shake the walls to tender, soul-stirring melodies that stop you in your tracks, he has mastered the full emotional spectrum of the instrument. Every string he touches tells a story, and every performance leaves the audience wanting more.

Cooking

In the kitchen, He approaches cooking like he approaches music, with creativity, boldness, and zero fear of experimentation. Whether he's slow-cooking a rich, hearty meal or throwing together something unexpected from whatever's in the fridge, the result is always something worth savoring.

Training

Kieran treats his body like an instrument, something to be disciplined, pushed, and constantly improved. He attacks his training with the same chaotic energy he brings to everything else in his life. For Kieran, the gym isn't just a place to get fit, it's where he clears his mind, builds his resilience, and fuels the fire that drives everything he does.
