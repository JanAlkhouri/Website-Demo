Jan Alkhouri

Introduction

Meet John, a 21-year-old from Syria with a passion for creativity and technology. He’s a computer science student who loves diving deep into complex problems and building solutions that matter. But coding isn’t his only language—John expresses himself through music production, crafting beats and melodies that blend technical precision with pure emotion. His guitar is never far away, and whether he’s working on fingerstyle arrangements or exploring new sounds in the studio, music is woven into everything he does. Beyond the screen and the strings, John is an avid reader who believes in continuously learning and growing. For him, it’s all about balancing the logical side of his mind with his creative spirit, and finding inspiration in every project he takes on.

Interests and hobbies

Music Production

He’s really into music production, constantly experimenting with sounds and pushing his creativity to the limit. He knows how to blend technical skills with artistic vision, turning raw ideas into polished tracks. It’s fascinating how he balances mixing, arranging, and all those technical details while keeping the emotion alive in every piece.

Hacking Nasa

He’s passionate about computer science and loves tackling complex problems with logic and innovation. Whether he’s building applications, optimizing systems, or exploring new technologies, he’s always thinking about how code can make a real impact. It’s clear he enjoys the challenge of creating something that actually matters.

Playing Guitar

He’s a dedicated guitarist who’s always working to improve his craft. From intricate fingerstyle arrangements to powerful solos, he puts real emotion into every note. You can tell he loves the continuous journey of mastering the instrument and expressing himself through music.
